About Eatto

Eatto is a food delivery platform that connects customers with nearby restaurants and independent delivery partners. Customers can browse menus, place orders, and track deliveries in real time using our mobile app and web portal.

Our goal is to provide a simple, transparent way for restaurants to receive online orders and for delivery partners to earn income on a flexible schedule.

How Eatto Works

  • Customers browse local restaurants, select items, and place orders through the Eatto app or website.
  • Restaurants receive the order details on their Eatto Partner app / dashboard and prepare the food.
  • Delivery partners use the Eatto Delivery app to accept orders, pick up from the restaurant, and deliver to the customer.
  • Payments are processed securely through integrated payment gateways. Restaurants and delivery partners receive payouts based on completed orders and agreed commission rates.

Restaurant & Delivery Partners

Restaurant Partners

Restaurants can sign up as Eatto partners to receive online orders without building their own ordering system. We provide:

  • Partner onboarding and menu setup
  • Order management dashboard
  • Sales reports and payout summaries

Delivery Partners

Delivery partners (riders) work on a commission basis. Eatto provides:

  • Access to delivery requests in their area
  • Transparent earnings per delivery
  • Support for account and payment issues
